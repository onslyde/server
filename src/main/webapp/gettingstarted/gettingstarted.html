Getting Started - Presentation

You can review the different features of onslyde with this demo. However, other people may be using it at the same time, so if things don't work like you think they should, register and create a private presentation. For the full story behind this project, check out our article on Smashing Magazine.

Setting up a presentation is simple. The form below is simply a kick starter to help you create an interactive presentation. After you view the presentation, you must download it and edit it on your computer. You can then open and review it in your browser.
